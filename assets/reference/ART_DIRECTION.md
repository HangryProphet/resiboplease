# Resibo, Please — Approved Art Direction

The approved reference is `approved_style_sheet.png`, derived from the visual
language of `mockup.png` and adapted for the fictional Bayhaven setting.

## Locked visual rules

- Hand-inked editorial caricature with thick, variable dark outlines.
- Clean cel shading, warm printed-poster color, and subtle paper grain.
- Playful but respectful adult proportions; no childish or anime styling.
- Core palette: navy, cream, burnt red, teal, mustard gold, and purple.
- Characters must be original and must not resemble real politicians.
- Raster generation is reserved for portraits, scenes, textures, and decorative
  artwork. Text, controls, data visualizations, and precise icons remain code.
- No candidate recommendation, match percentage, or hidden score may be baked
  into artwork.
